Tips & Tricks

  • Control the center early — it gives your pieces more power.
  • Develop all your minor pieces before moving the same piece twice.
  • Castle early to protect your king and connect your rooks.
  • Don’t rush pawn moves — every pawn move creates weaknesses.
  • Look for forcing moves: checks, captures, and threats.
  • Before every move, ask: “What changed after my opponent’s move?”
  • When you see a good move… look for a better one.
  • Trade pieces when ahead — keep pieces when behind.
  • Always think about your opponent’s plan, not just your own.
